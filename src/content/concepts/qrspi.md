# QRSPI

QRSPI is a methodology for breaking down complex tasks into stages: Question, Research, Structure, Plan, and Implement. It helps agents work more systematically.

## Why it matters

Rather than giving an AI one massive prompt, QRSPI encourages breaking work into focused stages. This leads to better outcomes and more maintainable workflows.

## How PromptStash uses it

PromptStash's stage feature is based on QRSPI principles. You can tag prompts with stages and compose them into bundles that follow this workflow.
