# Dumb Zone

The Dumb Zone refers to the point where an AI model's context is too full, leading to degraded performance. This typically happens when using more than 40-60% of a model's context window.

## Why it matters

When agents enter the Dumb Zone, they become less effective at following instructions and reasoning about code. Keeping instructions concise helps agents stay sharp.

## How PromptStash uses it

PromptStash shows token estimates and context budget gauges so you can keep your prompts and bundles within the smart zone.
