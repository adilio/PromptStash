# Bundles

Bundles are ordered collections of prompts that work together. Think of them as complete instruction sets for AI agents, combining multiple modules into a single export.

## Why it matters

Breaking complex instructions into smaller, focused modules makes them easier to maintain and reuse. Bundles let you compose these modules into complete agent configurations.

## How PromptStash uses it

Create a bundle, add prompts to it in a specific order, and export the whole thing as a single AGENTS.md or CLAUDE.md file.
