# Stages

Stages are a way to tag prompts as part of a structured workflow. They help organize prompts into logical phases like Research, Planning, Implementation, and Review.

## Why it matters

Stage typing makes it easier to compose prompts into bundles and ensures that all aspects of a workflow are covered. It's inspired by QRSPI (Question, Research, Structure, Plan, Implement) methodologies.

## How PromptStash uses it

You can tag prompts with stages like "Research", "Plan", or "Implement" to organize them. Later, you can compose these staged prompts into bundles that represent complete workflows.
